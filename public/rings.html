Rings

Rings. That ubiquitous symbol of commitment and eternity. Just like you, Plukka rings are matchless in terms of style and creativity. Our array of rings in diamonds, 18K gold, gemstones and sterling silver reflect our philosophy of choosing only the timeless best. Choose a ring for yourself or for another that truly reflects the power of commitment. For different moods and different occasions, you'll find our collection crammed with choices, from come-hither cocktail rings in the best tradition of Elizabeth Taylor to the delicate close-to-the skin bands that express your heart's quiet secrets. Wave your hand with a rainbow selection of gemstones of every single color and stone imaginable. From ever-popular blue topaz to rarefied pink diamonds you can let your true colors show with Plukka.
